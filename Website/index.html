Gaming

I like playing mostly older video games, modern games just don't do it for me. The focus on stripping content from finished titles and releasing it later under the title of "DLC", or simply reskinning an old game and re-releasing it is something that I don't find enticing. That's not to say I don't play current games, I will fire up the odd new game to play for a bit. My favourite is the S.T.A.L.K.E.R. series, have completed those games many times, with Shadow of Chernobyl being my favourite.
